Digital Transformation in HSE (Health, Safety & Environment) for the Oil & Gas industry means using digital technologies, data, automation, and intelligent systems to improve:

  1. worker safety
  2. operational safety
  3. environmental protection
  4. regulatory compliance
  5. emergency response
  6. risk prediction and prevention

in upstream, midstream, downstream, offshore, refinery, and petrochemical operations.
